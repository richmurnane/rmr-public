Our Mission

The Mission of the Rappahannock Model Railroaders, Inc., a nonprofit organization, is to reach out to the general public at private and community events to share the rewards and enjoyment of model railroading with kids of all ages through workshops, seminars and operating displays.
Our vision is to increase awareness in our community on the history of railroading in America to include model railroading and the enjoyment of collecting and operating toy trains.
