RMR Clubhouse

RMR Clubhouse

RMR maintains a permanent fixed 430 sq ft O & S gauge (the upper level has O&S dual gauge track) layout in our clubhouse. The perfect solution for those who do not have the room at home for a proper layout but are interested in the hobby.

As of March 2020, RMR is also actively constructing an HO gauge layout and considering an N scale layout.

The RMR clubhouse is located at 406 Hudgins Road in Fredericksburg VA.
If you are heading south on RT 1 from Fredericksburg: Turn right at the light immediately after Shirlie Slack Mitsubishi and then make the 2nd left (about even with the back of the Mitsubishi building). RMR is the 3rd door on your left. If you are heading north on RT 1 from Spotsylvania: Turn left at the light immediately after Sheehy Toyota and then make the 2nd left (about even with the back of the Mitsubishi building). RMR is the 3rd door on your left.
