Other Events

Monthly Meetings

The monthly RMR business meetings are held on the first Thursday of each month at the Salem Branch Public Library from 7:00 to 8:30 PM, Conference Room B. This meeting is open to the public, and dates/locations are subject to change upon availability of the space.
Club members often meet for dinner at a local restaurant prior to the monthly meeting to socialize.

Meetups

RMR Members socialize often with informal meetings at the clubhouse for model train "fun runs".
The club has an annual "Club Dinner" which is hosted at a local venue/restaurant.
Club members connect at various other times for trips and other social events.
